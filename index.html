About the Project

The Oman Wikipedia Generator is a national cultural archive project. Using the OMAN EVENTS MODEL, it transforms structured data about events, venues, festivals, creators, and programs into neutral, encyclopedic articles. This AI-powered engine supports both English and Arabic, making Omani heritage accessible worldwide and preserving our stories for future generations.
