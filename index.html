About the Project

The Oman Wikipedia Generator is a national cultural archive project. Using the Oman Events Model, it transforms structured data about events, venues, festivals, creators and heritage into comprehensive, neutral and multilingual articles that preserve our cultural legacy.

By combining structured metadata with AI, we ensure Oman’s stories are documented with precision and shared across languages and platforms.
